- About Me

I'm Deyondre, a graphic designer and amatuer Web Developer who made the leap from Saint Lucia to the United States. In the past few years I have been working on my craft and trying to make improvments. Originally a digital artist, My creative journey began in 2015 when I wanted to find a new hobby since I was bored at home. I picked up digital art and soon realized that I had a calling for this field. As time went by, I evolved from digital art and got my feet wet in graphic design. Currently I am in a new stage of my creative journey where I am learning. UI/UX Design & Web Development
Currently, I am a Computer Science student at a small college in New Jersey planning to transfer to a State University where my main focuses will be Computer Science and Visual Arts/ Graphic Design When it comes to skills, I would like to consider myself proficient in programs such as Photoshop, Illustrator, VSCode and also basic Office Programs Skills on the other hand, i'm good with HTML/CSS/Javascript and currently learning the React framework. Since i'm also a graphic designer, I'm learning UI/UX design also paired with a bit of branding. When I'm not sitting in-front a computer screen working, I'm usually photogaphy.
